### LLM Usage

For editing purposes, I use LLMs, but the content itself is not hallucinated or inspired by LLM output.
The main task LLMs are used for is grammar correction, because English isn't my native language.
Content is (and will be) free from any form of GAN-generated images/video/audio.

So if you decide to cook something from the [cooking](/cooking) section be sure it's not hallucinated,
but a real recipe made by humans.
